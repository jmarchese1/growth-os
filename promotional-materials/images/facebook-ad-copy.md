## Image Creation Notes

For actual image production, use these tools:
- **Canva** (canva.com) — Templates for Facebook ads, drag-and-drop
- **Figma** — Pixel-perfect designs matching Embedo brand
- **Midjourney/DALL-E** — AI-generated hero images for each concept
- **Adobe Express** — Quick social ad templates

### Embedo Brand Colors for Images
- Primary: `#6366F1` (Indigo)
- Violet: `#7C3AED`
- Dark BG: `#0c0a18`
- Green CTA: `#22c55e`
- White text on dark backgrounds
- Font: Plus Jakarta Sans (Google Fonts)

### Facebook Ad Image Specs
- Recommended size: 1200 x 628px (landscape) or 1080 x 1080px (square)
- Keep text under 20% of image area
- Use high contrast for mobile readability
- Include Embedo logo in corner
